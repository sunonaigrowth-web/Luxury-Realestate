About Sunon RealEstate

Building trust in luxury real estate through verified property listings, ownership validation, and transparent property information.

Our Story

Sunon RealEstate was created with a simple mission: to eliminate fake property listings and misleading information from the real estate market.


Many buyers waste valuable time visiting properties that are incorrectly described, already sold, or not genuinely available. We believe buyers deserve accurate information before making one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives.

Our Mission

To create the most trusted network of verified luxury property listings in Delhi NCR by physically inspecting every property before publication.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted premium property verification platform, where every listing is accurate, transparent and backed by real-world verification.
